# Menlo Ventures — State of GenAI in the Enterprise (2025)

## Summary
Annual enterprise GenAI survey from Menlo Ventures. Key finding for the CMM: 16% of enterprises have deployed true AI agents (tools that take actions, not just respond). The remainder are in chat-based or copilot-style deployment. Enterprise spend is climbing but concentrated in a handful of foundation-model vendors; integration work and governance lag spend.

## Why it matters for the CMM
Empirical anchor for "most organizations are at Level 1 or early Level 2." The 16% agent-deployment figure is the closest public measurement of Level 3+ prevalence. It also validates the current lede framing that the shift from chatbots to agents has happened unevenly — a minority of enterprises are doing it intentionally; a majority are absorbing it through employee initiative.

## Claims citable from this observation
- 16% of enterprises have deployed true AI agents as of the 2025 survey (Menlo Ventures)
- Enterprise GenAI spend continues to grow year-over-year but is concentrated on foundation models; integration and governance spend lags
- Copilot-style deployment remains the dominant enterprise pattern
